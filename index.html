Movie Ranking Project

The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Howl's Moving Castle

After a curse transforms a young woman named Sophie into an old lady, she seeks refuge in a wandering magical castle owned by a vain wizard named Howl, eventually discovering the power of self-confidence and love.

Kimi no Na wa (Your Name)

Is a breathtaking supernatural drama that follows two teenagers, Mitsuha and Taki, who mysteriously begin swapping bodies across time and space, eventually racing to prevent a looming disaster and find one another in person.

Weathering with You (Tenki no Ko)

Follows a runaway high school boy who befriends a "sunshine girl" capable of clearing the rainy Tokyo skies with a prayer, only to discover that her miraculous power comes with a tragic, world-altering cost.
